Methodology
How We Define Value for This Program

ROI for this program is not defined in dollars alone — success is measured through three lenses:

Lens 1 · Clinical
AWV & Preventive Completion
Increased Annual Wellness Visits and preventive screening completions — the direct gateway to the care cascade (COL, BCS, dental, A1c, flu vaccine) and HEDIS measure closure.
Lens 2 · Quality
CAHPS/HOS Score Movement
Improved member experience and health perception. Even 0.5★ movement in Medicare Advantage Star Ratings translates to $2–$5 PMPM in CMS bonus payments — the primary contractual outcome for this SOW.
Lens 3 · Population
Disengaged Member Reduction
Converting historically disengaged Medicare members into active health plan participants. Re-engagement is Goal #1 of this program — it is the prerequisite for every other outcome on this dashboard.
